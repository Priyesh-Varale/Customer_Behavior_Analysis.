# 🛍️ Customer Behavior Analysis | Python, SQL & Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer Behavior Analysis is an end-to-end Data Analytics project developed using **Power BI, SQL, and Python (Jupyter)** to analyze customer purchasing behavior, revenue trends, subscription patterns, product categories, and customer demographics. The project provides actionable business insights through interactive dashboards and analytical reports to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project is to:

- Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
- Monitor revenue and sales performance across product categories.
- Understand customer subscription trends.
- Identify high-performing customer segments.
- Evaluate customer demographics and purchasing patterns.
- Enable business decisions through interactive analytics.

## 📈 Key Insights

- Analyzed approximately **3,900 customer records**.
- Clothing generated the highest revenue and sales.
- Most customers were non-subscribers.
- Young Adult customers contributed the highest revenue.
- Customer review ratings averaged **3.75**.
- Purchase behavior varied across product categories and demographics.
